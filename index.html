The LinguaConnect Journey

Experience seamless language exchange from start to finish with our 18-step process.

Step 1

Splash / Logo Screen

A quick animated intro welcoming you to LinguaConnect.

Step 2

Signup/Login Screen

Securely log in via Email or Google to access your account.

Step 3

Language Selection

Choose your native language and the language you want to practice.

Step 4

Dashboard / Waitlist

Review match preferences (age, gender, level). If offline, join a waitlist.

Step 5

Active Matching

The system actively searches for your ideal language partner.

Step 6

Partner Found Alert

Get notified instantly when a matching speaker is ready.

Step 7

Pre-Call Screen

1-minute window to accept or decline the upcoming session.

Step 8

Audio Call UI

Basic audio session with mute, speaker, and disconnect options.

Step 9

10-Min Timer

A balanced 5+5 minute split ensures both users get practice time.

Step 10

Guided Prompts

Access provided conversation starters if you run out of things to say.

Step 11

Report/Block Feature

Easily flag inappropriate behavior during the session.

Step 12

End Call / Transition

Post-call prompt asking if you'd like to text the partner further.

Step 13

Mutual Validation

Both users must approve to unlock permanent text messaging.

Step 14

Messaging Interface

Text your new language partner securely within the app.

Step 15

Grammar Correction

Send and receive inline grammar corrections on messages.

Step 16

Settings/Profile

Manage your account, preferences, and subscriptions.

Step 17

Past Connections

View history of users you've previously talked or matched with.

Step 18

Premium Paywall

Upgrade to unlock unlimited practice and priority matching.
